# Story Content Development Plan

## Objective
Develop a complete 5-day erotic narrative arc with full choice descriptors, atmospheric text, and psychological tension. Tone: sexy, fun, erotic, and dominating.

## Structure
Each day has:
- Opening atmospheric setup
- Choice A (early/midday) with 2 options
- Choice B (evening) with 2 options
- Transitional narrative that flows into next day

## Days & Themes

### Day 1: The Literary Tease
**Theme**: Mental stimulation, shared imagination, mood-setting through narrative
- Choice A: Genre Selection (slow-burn Victorian vs. modern spicy)
- Choice B: Reading Environment (passive presence vs. interactive rules)

### Day 2: Digital Voyeurism
**Theme**: Visual longing, distance play, voyeuristic tension
- Choice A: Lighting & presentation
- Choice B: Director's role (silent observer vs. commanding)

### Day 3: Edge of Control
**Theme**: Kink exploration, sensory play, orgasmic denial
- Choice A: Sensory deprivation (blindfold vs. restraints)
- Choice B: Sensation play (temperature vs. denial)

### Day 4: Elegance & Inhibition
**Theme**: Power of desire, public/private tension, restraint and anticipation
- Choice A: Wardrobe (minimal vs. luxe)
- Choice B: Kissing style (public risk vs. intimate intensity)

### Day 5: Grand Finale
**Theme**: Total release, no boundaries, culmination
- Choice A: Setting (raw intensity vs. intentional sanctuary)
- Choice B: Control dynamic (surrender vs. taking)

## Writing Standards
- Atmospheric, evocative language
- Mix intellectual and sensual elements
- Implied eroticism alongside explicit intent
- Tone: confident, commanding, but consensual and playful
- Keep pacing varied: buildup, intensity, breath-taking moments
- Avoid clinical language; favor sensory details
